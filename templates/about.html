Take a Paws

From one anxious dog lover to another. Life can be a lot, and sometimes we just need to pause, take a moment and look at pictures of dogs.

My own dog, Ember, has changed my life more than I can ever explain and is the reason I smile most days.

It is proven that dogs can have a positive impact on human's mental health, and they teach us so much about living in the moment and taking things slow.

"Dogs have a way of finding the people who need them, and filling the emptiness we didn't know we had."
